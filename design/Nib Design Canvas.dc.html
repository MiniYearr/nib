Nib — the extensible notepad

A local-first, plugin-based "everything notepad" — part notepad, part to-do system, part locked diary, with a small AI companion that lives on the screen. Every feature, including the core ones, is a toggleable module over one shared data layer.

# Q3 planning
Edited 3:12 PM · 412 words · autosaved

Three bets this quarter, and we cut everything else. The through-line is the shared data layer — once search, tags, and memory all read from it, every module gets smarter for free.

## The three bets
1.Recurrence builder — arbitrary rules, not just "daily". Ships first.
2.Unified index — one query across notes, tasks & diary.
3.Plugin SDK v2 — third parties register modules & memory hooks.
